Welcome to The Karl Marx’s Press Review! A brand new service finally providing the solution for your greatest desire: to know Karl Marx’s opinion on the latest events!

A Marxist Generative Transformer

Karl Marx was one of the punchiest economist and political theorist of all times. Unfortunately, the man is also very dead, and despite all our efforts we did not (yet) manage to contact him in the Afterlife.
This is why, as a backup solution in the meanwhile, we decided to teach a Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT-2) to speak like the philosopher.

In case you’re wondering, a Generative Pre-trained Transformer is not a giant Japanese robot that can change to some funky vehicle and back again. We would have loved that too, but no. A Generative Pre-trained Transformer is an unsupervised language model. Generally speaking, given a textual input, a GPT generates coherent paragraphs by predicting in turn the word which comes next. Researchers had GPT-2 learn English by training it on millions of webpages. As for us, we have limited ourselves to guide our Transformer through an “educational” journey across Marx’s (and Engel’s) most important works.
Our GPT-2 is now a pretty opinionated one, if not always very intelligent. It will keep you good company while we try and contact the original Marx.
